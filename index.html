Poop complaints have swelled in all San Francisco neighborhoods — except this one

REPORTING • ANALYSIS • DATAVIZ • SFCHRONICLE
I downloaded millions of rows of data from the city’s 311 requests database, implemented cleaning and recoding strategies using R, and produced three Datawrapper visualizations to enhance readers' understanding of the data, including a map of feces-related calls by neighborhood.

U.S. Citizens twice as likely to be charged with crime in Harris County, data shows

ANALYSIS • DEMOGRPAPHICS • REPORTING • DATAVIZ • HOUSTONLANDING
I analyzed Harris County criminal charge data and found that U.S. citizens were more than twice as likely to be charged with a crime in 2023 than adults who don’t hold U.S. citizenship.
